* Normal Distribution
** Formula
\begin{equation}
f(x)=e^{{-\frac{1}{2}}\big({\frac{x-m}{\sigma}}\big)^{2}}\frac{1}{\sqrt{2\pi}\sigma}
\end{equation}
- If X is a normal variate with parameter m,\sigma,then
  \begin{equation}
  Z=\frac{X-m}{\sigma}
  \end{equation}
  is also a normal variate with mean = 0 and standard deviation = 1.
** Mean and Variance
*** Mean
\begin{equation}
Mean=m
\end{equation}
*** Variance
- $Variance=\sigma^{2}$
- $\sigma=\sqrt{npq}$
- $q=1-p$
- $p=\frac{m}{100}$
